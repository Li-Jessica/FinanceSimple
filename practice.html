Use These Flashcards to Practice Your Financial Literacy!

What is a Savings Account?

A deposit account in a bank or credit union that pays interest on your balance

What is a Checking Account?

Transaction accounts that let you authorize the transfer of money to other people or organizations by writing a check or electronic transfer.

What is Debt?

An obligation to repay an amount you owe.

What is Interest?

What you pay to borrow money using a loan, credit card, or line of credit.

What is Credit Score?

A rating used by credit reporting companies to help lenders decide whether and/or how much credit can be extended to a borrower.

What Factors Affect Credit?

Length of credit history, types of credit, how much you owe, payment history.
